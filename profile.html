Terms and Conditions

Last updated: 12 August, 2025

QuickKart ("hum", "hamara", "app") istemal karne ke liye aapka dhanyavad. Hamari service istemal karke, aap neeche diye gaye niyamon aur sharton se sehmat hain:

1. Services

QuickKart ek online platform hai jo aapko apne aas-paas ki dukaan se grocery aur anya zaroori saaman khareedne aur uski delivery paane ki suvidha deta hai.

2. User Account

Order karne ke liye, aapko apna naam aur ek valid 10-digit mobile number dekar account banana hoga. Aap apne account ki suraksha ke liye khud zimmedar hain.

3. Orders aur Payment

  • Sabhi orders par ₹50 ka minimum order value laagu hai.
  • Payment abhi ke liye "Cash on Delivery" (COD) ke maadhyam se hi uplabdh hai.
  • Order place karne ke baad, hum use confirm karenge. Hum kisi bhi order ko cancel karne ka adhikar rakhte hain.

4. Delivery

  • Hamari delivery service ek seemit kshetr (5 km ke daayre) mein hi uplabdh hai.
  • Delivery fee aapki location ke adhaar par calculate ki jayegi aur order summary mein dikhayi jayegi.

5. Referral aur Coupons

Aap hamare referral program ka istemal karke coupons kama sakte hain. In coupons ko aap apne orders par discount paane ke liye istemal kar sakte hain. Coupons se jude niyam app mein bataye jayenge.

6. Prtibandhit Utpaad (Restricted Products)

Hum Google Play Store ki neetiyon ka paalan karte hain. Isliye, tambaku, cigarette, ya koi bhi pratibandhit utpaad is app ke zariye nahi becha jayega.

7. Niyam aur Sharton mein Badlav

Hum in niyam aur sharton ko samay-samay par badal sakte hain. Kisi bhi badlav ki jaankari aapko de di jayegi.

8. Sampark Karein (Contact Us)

Agar aapka koi sawaal hai, to aap humein yahan sampark kar sakte hain: ofassam686@gmail.com

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 August, 2025

Aapki privacy hamare liye bahut zaroori hai. Yeh policy batati hai ki hum aapki kaun si jaankari lete hain aur use kaise istemal karte hain.

1. Hum Kaun si Jaankari Lete Hain?

  • Personal Information: Aapka naam aur phone number.
  • Location Information: Aapki aaspas ki location (GPS coordinates) taaki hum delivery kar sakein.
  • Order Information: Aapne kya-kya saaman order kiya hai, uski details.

2. Hum Jaankari Ka Istemal Kaise Karte Hain?

  • Order Process Karne Ke Liye: Aapka order taiyaar karne aur aap tak pahunchane ke liye.
  • Aapse Sampark Karne Ke Liye: Aapke order se judi jaankari dene ya kisi samasya ke liye aapse phone par baat karne ke liye.
  • Delivery Ke Liye: Aapki location ka istemal karke delivery address confirm karne aur delivery fee calculate karne ke liye.

3. Data Sharing

Hum aapki personal jaankari kisi teesre paksh (third-party) ke saath bechte ya share nahi karte hain. Aapka address sirf delivery partner ko diya jaata hai.

4. Data Suraksha

Aapka saara data Firebase ke surakshit servers par store kiya jaata hai. Hum aapke data ko surakshit rakhne ke liye zaroori kadam uthate hain.

5. Policy mein Badlav

Agar hum apni privacy policy mein koi badlav karte hain, to uski jaankari hum aapko de denge.
